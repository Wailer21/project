About the business

Aggie's Nail Studio Shop is a registered small e-commerce business based in Maseru, Lesotho. The business specializes in high-quality press-on nail sets, nail care products, and accessories. Since opening, the shop has focused on providing beautiful, ready-to-wear nails that customers can easily apply at home.

The business was founded with a mission to make professional-looking nails accessible and affordable. Every product is carefully selected, inspected for quality, and packaged with care before delivery. Our Shop serves customers across Maseru and selected delivery areas in Lesotho.

What customers can expect

  • Clear information: All prices are shown in Lesotho Maloti (LSL) with no hidden fees
  • Quality assurance: Every product is checked before shipping or pickup
  • Customer support: Questions are answered promptly via email or WhatsApp
  • Fast confirmation: Orders are confirmed within 24 hours
  • Secure transactions: Payment options include cash on pickup and bank transfer

What the business sells

  • Press-on nail sets (short and medium lengths)
  • Nail glue and basic nail tools
  • Cuticle oil (10ml bottles)
  • Moisturizing hand cream
  • Nail art add-on services for press-ons
  • Gift cards (available upon request)

Business model

Nail Studio Shop operates as a direct-to-customer e-commerce business. Revenue is generated through product sales at a profit margin (selling price minus cost price). Additional services like custom nail art and delivery fees provide supplementary income.

  • Customers browse products on the Shop page
  • Orders are placed using the email order form
  • The business confirms payment and arranges delivery or pickup
  • No physical storefront — all operations are online and appointment-based

Press-on aftercare tips (from the business)

  • Prep natural nails (clean and dry) before applying press-ons.
  • Avoid water for 1–2 hours after application to allow glue to set.
  • Do not pull press-ons off — soak in warm soapy water to remove.
  • Use cuticle oil daily to maintain healthy natural nails.
  • Store unused press-ons in a cool, dry place.
