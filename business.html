Business Model

Nail Studio Shop operates as a small e-commerce business. Below is how the business works and earns revenue.

How the business earns money

  • Product profit margin: Each product is sold at a price higher than its cost (selling price minus cost price = profit).
  • Add-on services: Nail art, custom designs, and special shapes increase the price because they take extra time and materials.
  • Delivery fees: Fees depend on the customer's location and distance from Maseru.
  • Bulk orders: Discounts are offered for bulk purchases, but the business still maintains a small profit per unit.

Business costs (expenses)

  • Product costs: Buying press-on nails, nail glue, cuticle oil, hand cream, and tools from suppliers.
  • Packaging materials: Boxes, bubble wrap, and thank-you cards for each order.
  • Marketing: Social media ads and promotional materials (optional).
  • Delivery: Transportation costs for delivering orders to customers.
  • Miscellaneous: Phone credit for customer communication and small supplies.

Pricing strategy

  • Press-on set (Short): M120 - Basic set with file included
  • Press-on set (Medium): M150 - Longer length, shape options available
  • Cuticle oil (10ml): M40 - Essential nail care product
  • Hand cream (small): M35 - Moisturizing hand cream
  • Nail glue: M25 - For press-on application
  • Nail art add-on: M30+ - Depends on design complexity
  • Delivery fee: Varies by location (calculated per order)

Prices are in Lesotho Maloti (LSL) and include basic packaging. Custom orders may be priced differently.

Order & payment process

  1. Customer browses products on the Shop page.
  2. Customer fills out the order form with product, quantity, and delivery preference.
  3. Order is sent via email to the business.
  4. Business responds within 24 hours with total price (including delivery if applicable).
  5. Customer makes payment via cash on pickup or bank transfer.
  6. Once payment is confirmed, the order is prepared.
  7. Customer picks up in Maseru or delivery is arranged.

Target customers

  • Women aged 18-40 who enjoy beauty and nail care
  • Busy professionals who want ready-to-wear press-on nails
  • Beginners learning to do nails at home
  • Gift buyers looking for affordable beauty gifts
  • Local customers in Maseru and surrounding areas

Delivery & pickup policy

  1. Pickup: Free pickup in Maseru at an arranged time.
  2. Delivery: Available in selected areas. Fee depends on distance.
  3. Processing time: Orders are ready within 1-2 business days after payment.
  4. Order confirmation: Customers receive a message with pickup/delivery details.

Future business goals

  • Expand product range to include more nail art tools
  • Offer subscription boxes for repeat customers
  • Partner with local salons for bulk orders
  • Create a website with online payment integration
  • Expand delivery to more areas in Lesotho
